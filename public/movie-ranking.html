The Best Movies According to Keda

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shawshank Redemption

This might just be my favourite movie! Being put to prison for something you haven't done is just saddening but getting your part of justice at the end is just a wholesome thing.

Hacksaw Ridge

This is my favourite gospel genre movie. The character played by Andrew Garfield in this is amazing. Imagine wanting to be in a war zone without any guns but to be a medic just for the zeal to save the others on the field. Even better, he eventually goes on to lead the army in prayer just before entering into the war zone. A must-watch!

Life is Beautiful

This movie revolves around the time of the dictatorship of the Nazis over the Jews. A funny and loving father makes up stories for his son while they were held captives inorder to keep his son away from worry. He pretends to be happy and excited whenever he is around his son. The sacrifices a father can make for their children!
